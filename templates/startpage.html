Know-Thyself

Real-world Integration for Student Empowerment

Know-Thyself is an experiential learning platform designed to prepare students for the world of work—long before they graduate. Here, students explore realistic job postings, craft and upload their resumes, and experience the process of applying to roles relevant to their field of study.

  • Reflect – Clarity on your career interests and job market expectations
  • Identify – Hands-on practice in writing professional resumes and cover letters
  • Seek – Interview readiness aligned with real job descriptions
  • Evolve – Opportunities tailored to psychology students
